Our Story

From Passion to Profession

Founded in 2018, Team ABH PK began as a small group of gaming enthusiasts who shared a common passion for competitive esports. What started as casual gaming sessions soon evolved into a professional organization as we began participating in national tournaments.

Recognizing the growing digital landscape in Pakistan, we expanded our focus to include software development and content creation. Today, we operate at the intersection of technology and entertainment, delivering innovative solutions while maintaining our competitive edge in esports.

Our Mission & Values

We believe in pushing boundaries and challenging the status quo. Our mission is to:

  • Develop software that solves real-world problems
  • Foster Pakistan's growing esports community
  • Create educational content about technology and gaming
  • Provide opportunities for young talent in tech and gaming
