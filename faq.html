Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gaming team, apps, and services.

General Questions

What is Team ABH PK?

Team ABH PK is Pakistan's premier gaming and content creation team. We specialize in competitive esports (particularly PUBG Mobile and Free Fire), game development, and creating engaging gaming content for our community.

How can I join Team ABH PK?

We regularly hold tryouts for talented gamers and content creators. Requirements vary by position but generally include:

  • Proven skills in your game of choice (high rank/tournament experience)
  • Positive attitude and team spirit
  • Consistent streaming/content creation (for content creator roles)
  • Availability for practice and tournaments

Follow our social media for tryout announcements.

Where are you based and do you compete internationally?

Our headquarters is in Lahore, Pakistan, but we have players and creators across the country. We compete in both national and international tournaments, including:

  • PUBG Mobile Pakistan Series
  • Free Fire Pakistan Championship
  • Various international online tournaments
  • LAN events when possible
When was Team ABH PK founded?

Team ABH PK was officially founded in 2018 by a group of passionate gamers who wanted to create a professional esports organization in Pakistan. Since then, we've grown to become one of the country's most recognized gaming teams.

What games does your team specialize in?

Our primary competitive focus is on:

  • PUBG Mobile (Battlegrounds Mobile India)
  • Free Fire
  • Call of Duty Mobile
  • Valorant

We also have content creators who cover a wider variety of games including Minecraft, Among Us, and other popular titles.

Our Apps & Tools

What is Mrearn and how does it work?

Mrearn is our innovative app that lets users earn rewards by:

  1. Listening to Holy Books (Quran, Hadith, etc.)
  2. Watching short advertisements
  3. Completing simple tasks

Users accumulate points that can be redeemed for various rewards. It's our way of combining spiritual growth with practical benefits.

What is the AB2D Engine?

The AB2D Engine is our visual scripting tool designed to help aspiring game developers:

  • Create games without traditional coding
  • Prototype ideas quickly
  • Learn game development concepts
  • Export projects to multiple platforms

It's perfect for beginners and experienced developers who want to speed up their workflow.

Are your apps available on iOS?

Currently, our apps are only available for Android devices. We're working on iOS versions, but don't have a specific release date yet. Follow our social media for updates on iOS availability.

You can download our Android apps from our website or the Google Play Store.

How do I redeem my Mrearn rewards?

To redeem your Mrearn rewards:

  1. Accumulate at least 1000 points
  2. Go to the 'Rewards' section in the app
  3. Select your preferred reward option
  4. Complete the verification process
  5. Your reward will be processed within 3-5 business days

Reward options include mobile balance, gift cards, and charity donations.

Is the AB2D Engine free to use?

The AB2D Engine offers multiple tiers:

  • Free Version: Basic features with Team ABH PK branding
  • Pro Version ($9.99/month): Advanced features and exports
  • Enterprise Version: Custom solutions for studios

Students can apply for educational discounts through our website.

Competitive Gaming

What tournaments has Team ABH PK won?

Some of our notable tournament victories include:

  • 2023 PUBG Mobile Pakistan Championship (1st place)
  • Free Fire Pakistan Series Season 2 (Grand Finals)
  • Gaming Galaxy LAN Tournament 2022
  • Multiple ESL Pakistan online tournaments

We maintain an updated trophy case on our About page.

How do you select players for tournaments?

Our tournament rosters are determined by:

  1. Performance in scrims and practice matches
  2. Specialization for the specific tournament/game
  3. Team chemistry and communication skills
  4. Past tournament experience
  5. Coach and analyst recommendations

We rotate players to give everyone competitive experience.

Do you have a junior team or academy?

Yes! Team ABH PK Academy is our development program for:

  • Players aged 14-17
  • Emerging talent with competitive potential
  • Content creators building their audience

The academy provides structured training, mentorship, and opportunities to compete in junior tournaments. Applications open quarterly.
